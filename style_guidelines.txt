
Overall Look and Feel of Mana

==============================

We want Mana to be a tutoring service that empowers both students and tutors alike.

Our app will be boldly royal blue, with light blue, purple and white accents.

Our buttons will be flat.

The website should be energetic and professional for the users.

Iconography
============

We will have a flat design for all of our icons.

Typography
===========

We will be using Varela Round from google fonts as the primary font.

Links
======

Links are not underlined, but will change colour when hovered over. 

Buttons
========

Buttons will be flat and change colour when hovered over.

Pictures
=========

Stock photos will be from google.
